for the one who holds my heart

— in light, in colour, in truth —

you are my quiet place

In a world that moves too fast, your presence feels like a soft landing. It’s not about what we have — it’s that we have each other. You matter more than any gift I could give. If I owned the stars, I’d weave them into a dress just for you. Since I can’t, I offer you my full attention, my growth, my today and all my tomorrows.

honest & human

We’ve met, we’ve laughed, we’ve held each other — yet sometimes I feel I haven’t shown you the depth of what you mean to me. I carry you with me always. I am not perfect, but my love for you is real, and I want you to know: I am here, fully present, learning to love you better every day.

if i ever hurt you

From the bottom of my heart, I am sorry for any moment I made you feel less than cherished — knowingly or unknowingly. The last thing I want is to cause you pain. Your comfort and peace are sacred to me. Please forgive my human flaws; I am learning, always learning, with you.

i promise to try

I don’t promise perfection — I promise effort. I promise to listen, to show up, to be consistent. I will hold your heart gently and nurture what we’re building. With every sunrise, I want to give you a love that feels like safety, like home, like a soft place to land.

tell me anything

If there’s something you dislike, something you fear, something I do that hurts you — I am ready to listen. No pride, no walls. Your voice matters. I want you to feel free, because truth is the air our love breathes. There is no judgment here, only arms open wide.

“You are not just my everything — you are my best friend, my quiet hope, my favourite thought.”

with all sincerity and love,
Brian Kiprono
